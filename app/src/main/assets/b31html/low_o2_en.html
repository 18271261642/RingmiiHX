Hypoxia time

Medical examination explanation

The red area of ​​the chart is an abnormal hypoxia.

Noun explanation

The duration of a state of insufficient physiological oxygen or tissue oxygen demand.

Typical conditions

Dizziness and difficulty breathing.

Treatment methods

(1) Direct oxygen or aerobic exercise, including walking, jogging, deep breathing, and improving the oxygen inhalation environment will also help.

(2) Seek medical treatment if necessary, and the drug improves.
