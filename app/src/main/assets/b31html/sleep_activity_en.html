Amplitude of sleep activity

Medical examination explanation

When the amplitude of sleep activity is greater than 50, it can be determined as sleep agitation.

Noun explanation

refers to unconscious body movement during sleep.

Typical conditions

Turn around, turn more frequently at night, and sweat more.

Improvement plan

Pay attention to your diet and drink a glass of drink before going to bed, such as milk or millet porridge. Make some preparations before going to bed, such as soaking your feet in hot water,     Massage the foot's Yongquan and so on. Sleeping sideways can effectively reduce body movement. Improving the sleeping environment (indoor humidity, temperature) can also help.
